Sleevr

A Cable Sleeving Color Designer

Have you ever:

  • Created custom sleeves for your PC or electronics cables?
  • Attempted to use Photoshop or a similar tool to map out your designs before you attempt to start sleeving?
  • Drawn potential schemes on a sheet of paper, but not had the proper colors to reflect what you want?

If the answer to any of those questions is, "Yes, I totally have,"... or even if the answer is, "Nope, definitely not," this tool may be of use to you.

I personally have tried several different ways to map out sleeve designs, but they're all too tedious or inefficient. Look no further, than Sleevr!
